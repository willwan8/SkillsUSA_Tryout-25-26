Why join?

As a member, you’ll gain real-world experience through competitions, projects, and workshops. You’ll work alongside motivated students, develop leadership skills, and receive mentorship that prepares you for college and careers. No matter your background, you belong here.

Information to know

Cost

The membership fee for SkillsUSA is $225 for the Metea Valley Chapter, but only after you're a part of the state team. The fee includes the cost to go to state.

Commitment

There are meetings for different clusters every week. These meetings are around an hour. Depending on your event, you will need to do various levels of preparation before competitions.

Events

We offer a wide variety of events, which vary in clusters. There are events in engineering, computer science, healthcare, business, and other sectors as well. SkillsUSA offers over 110 events!
