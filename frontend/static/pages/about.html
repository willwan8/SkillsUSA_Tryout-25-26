Our Story

Metea Valley’s SkillsUSA chapter was created to help students build real-world skills, explore career paths, and grow as leaders. It brings together motivated students who want hands-on experience, community involvement, and a place to represent Metea with pride.

Values

Integrity

We demonstrate honesty and strong moral principles in every project, competition, and collaboration.

Respect

We value a positive, inclusive environment where every student’s ideas, backgrounds, and talents are honored.

Responsibility

Our students take ownership of their work, meeting deadlines and display professionalism.

Citizenship

Members actively contribute to the school and community, showing their pride and commitment.

Service

We’re committed to building the community by fostering a friendly and nurturing environment.
